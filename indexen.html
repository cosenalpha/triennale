11 communication design projects, 11 narratives and non-human perspectives, 11 communication machines to reflect on the relationship of the human being with nature in the era of climate change.
Projects result from an educational experimentation path between communication design, speculative design, programming and making.
Interactive experiences that, through the reversal of the anthropocentric point of view, propose narratives, provocations and reflections on our era and the future.
